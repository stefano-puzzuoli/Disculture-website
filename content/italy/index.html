Italy

The Flag

The flag of Italy (bandiera d'Italia) is a tricolor flag - three equal vertical bands of green (hoist side), white, and red. Green Color on Italian Flag represents hope and joy, it also said to be symbolizes the grassy landscapes and the beauty of Italy. White color on Italian Flag represents peace and honesty as well as also said to be the symbol of white snowy areas of Italy. Red color on Italian Flag represents strength and the blood shed and sacrifices that were made for the sake of the country. The red and white colors were borrowed from the official colors of the Milanese flag, and later green was added to represent the Civic Guards of Milan. Green was also said to be Napaloeans favorite color and one of the main reasons it was added in Italian flag after he conquered Italy.
